Our Courses

Chinese for Beginners

Our beginner Chinese course is for students who have never studied the language before. This course provides an introduction to basic Mandarin Chinese and will help you function at a basic everyday survival level.

Intermediate Chinese Course

This course will help you become more confident in speaking Mandarin. At the completion of the intermediate course, you’ll be able to communicate at a considerably higher level with native speakers and handle personal, social, and day-to-day situations using appropriate structures.

Advanced Chinese Course

Advanced Chinese Course will help to gain solid knowledge of Mandarin to become as fluent as possible. At the completion of this course, you will be able to follow any debate, discussion or conversation and communicate with all situations at a near-native level.

Spoken Chinese Course

This course is mainly designed for any beginner to advanced level students who want to get better at Chinese speaking with precise pronunciation techniques. This course will allow speakers to become more professional at language handling and, moreover, suitable for any beginner, intermediate or advanced course followers to build up more confidence when using the language.

Business Chinese

This course focuses on developing Chinese language confidence and giving students a skill-set for high-level trade communication and relationship building. Our business Chinese course provides a valuable opportunity to explore different markets while sharing the experience of mastering a new language.

HSK Test Preparation Course

HSK stands for Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi (汉语水平考试) and it is the Chinese Language proficiency skill test developed by China all around the world to measure the Chinese Language fluency under Listening, reading, writing and speaking scopes in a standard method...
